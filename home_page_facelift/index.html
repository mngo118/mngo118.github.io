In Covid News:

Starting OCT 25, we will be requiring guests to be fully vaccinated. As per the governor's mandate, we will require guests of 12+ years to have/provide their vaccine cards or proof of negative covid test results upon entering.

Here is the list of accepted documents as per the government health website (please note this is only required for dine-in guests ages 12+):

  • Completed vaccination card with dates of both shots listed
  • Picture of completed vaccination card
  • Documentation of completed (both) vaccine shots
  • Negative covid results within 72 hours

We are very sorry for any inconvenience. We will be following the governor's guidelines because we care about you, our community, and our staff. We hope that you understand and help us keep our community safe.
